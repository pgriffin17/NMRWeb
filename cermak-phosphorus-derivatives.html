Faculty Research
Dr. Diana Cermak
This project is a collaboration with chemists at the U.S. Department of Agriculture, Agricultural Research Service (USDA-ARS), Bio-Oils Research (BOR) Group in Peoria, IL, dealing with the synthesis of phosphorus derivatives of castor and lesquerella oils.

We are working to convert the primary naturally-occurring fatty acids found in castor oil (compound 5a) and lesquerella oil (compound 5b) into a variety of phosphorus derivatives, such as compounds 6, 7, and 8, which may have interesting biological activity.
